Company History

This company was set up due to an increasing demand from industrial boiler companies for specific training. This has come about because there is a lack of trained engineers in the workplace with the required skills sets. We have carried out extensive research on the skills sets required by these companies. We have custom designed our courses to target these skill sets and provide the companies with the training they require.

The company initially started offering online training courses, most of which were very successful. It became obvious from an early stage that some courses could not be completed on line and this highlighted the need for practical training courses to be made availiable.

Training Courses

The company offers a wide veriety of training courses tailored to your needs.

One of our experienced training course design engineers will visit your company and carry out a detailed survey. This will include monitoring all operational practices and procedures.

A detailed report will be drawn up, with input from both sides. We will the match you to relevant courses on our database, or a custom made training course will be designed by our engineers and approved by your company.

This way your workforce will aquire the exact skill sets required to become an asset to your company.
